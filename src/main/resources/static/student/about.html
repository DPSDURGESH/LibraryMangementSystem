About Library Management System

The Library Management System (LMS) is a web-based application designed to streamline the management of library resources, including books, journals and more. It provides a user-friendly interface for librarians, administrators to efficiently manage library operations.

Features of our Library Management System include:

  • Efficient book cataloging and inventory management
  • Member management
  • Search and retrieval of library resources

Our goal is to modernize library operations, improve user experience, and promote literacy and education within our community. The Library Management System is continually evolving to meet the changing needs of libraries and their users.
